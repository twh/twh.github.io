Abstract: We introduce PRISM, a multi-modal generative foundation model for slide-level histopathology. Rather than processing individual image tiles separately, PRISM operates at the whole-slide level using Virchow tile embeddings and clinical report text. The model demonstrates zero-shot cancer detection and sub-typing with performance comparable to supervised methods, while also showing strong results in biomarker prediction tasks with limited training data.
